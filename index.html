Section II ‚Äî Purpose

The Problem & The Purpose Behind Us

The "Maturity" Myth

Why Youth Voices Get Silenced

The primary challenge facing the youth today is the misconception that their advocacy is merely a form of rebellion. When young people speak up about climate change, social justice, or economic policy, their input is dismissed.

This dismissal ignores a critical reality: young people are not just leaders of tomorrow; they are active stakeholders of today who will inherit the long-term consequences of current decisions.

Our project, "Not Too Young to Speak", seeks to dismantle the stigma of "rebellion" and replace it with the framework of responsibility. Taking an interest in societal issues is an act of civic duty ‚Äî not an act of defiance.

In Cebu and across the Philippines, young people experience their opinions being ignored or dismissed in online forums, community gatherings, and even in households where they are discouraged from speaking up.
